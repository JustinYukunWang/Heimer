# Heimer: AI-Powered League of Legends Coach

## Vision

Heimer is an AI-powered coaching platform for League of Legends players.

Unlike traditional stat websites such as OP.GG, U.GG, or Mobalytics, Heimer aims to provide personalized coaching recommendations based on comparisons against high-ranked players and the user's own historical performance.

The long-term goal is to create a desktop application with:

- Player profile analysis
- Historical trend analysis
- Champion-specific coaching
- Benchmarking against Master+ players
- Post-game reviews
- Live in-game performance tracking
- AI-generated coaching recommendations

---

# Core Product Philosophy

Most League tools answer:

> "What happened?"

Heimer answers:

> "Why did it happen, and what should I improve next?"

Example:

Instead of:

> Vision Score: 30

Heimer says:

> Your average Vision Score at 30 minutes is 30.
> Master+ players on Aatrox average 42.
> This suggests insufficient vision setup before objective fights.
> Focus on establishing river vision 60–90 seconds before Dragon and Baron spawns.

# MVP 2: Player Scanner + Benchmark Comparison

## Goal

Scan any Riot account and compare their performance against Master+ benchmark data.
Ranked Solo/Duo only.

### User Flow

User enters:

```text
Game Name + Tag (e.g. TheKun#Wang)
```

System:

1. Fetches profile (icon, ID, tag, current rank, LP)
2. Fetches last 20 ranked games
3. Displays full profile and match history
4. User can filter by role and/or champion
5. After filtering: shows aggregated stats + per-minute breakdown vs Master+ benchmark

---

### Section 1: Player Profile Header

- Profile picture — fetched on-demand from DDragon using profileIconId returned
  by the Riot summoner API. URL pattern:
  ddragon.leagueoflegends.com/cdn/{version}/img/profileicon/{iconId}.png
  Not stored in DB.
- Game Name + Tag
- Current rank + LP
- Winrate (overall, ranked)
- Most played role

---

### Section 2: Match History (last 20 ranked games)

Per game:

- Champion played
- Role
- KDA
- CS
- Gold
- Win/Loss
- Game duration
- Items at end of game

---

### Section 3: Champion Pool

- Most played champions (by game count)
- Winrate per champion
- Average KDA per champion

---

### Section 4: Filtered Analysis (role and/or champion filter)

User selects a role (e.g. TOP) and/or champion (e.g. Aatrox).

Displays overall averages across filtered games:

- Winrate
- KDA
- CS/min
- Gold/min
- Total vision score (average per game — not per minute, Riot API limitation)
- Total damage dealt to champions (average per game — not per minute)

---

### Section 5: Per-Minute Stats (Star Feature)

At each timestamp (5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30 minutes), show the player's average
stats from their filtered games side-by-side against Master+ benchmark for the
same role and/or champion.

Example:

```text
10 Minutes — Top Lane Aatrox

             You        Master+    Diff
CS           58         72         -14
Gold         3,200      3,850      -650
Level        5          6          -1
KDA          1/1/0      1.2/0.8/1  —
XP           4,100      4,600      -500
```

Notes:
- Vision score and damage are NOT shown per minute (not available in Riot
  timeline API). Shown as overall game averages in Section 4 instead.
- Level is kept alongside XP — level differences are more intuitive for
  players (power spikes at 6/9/11/16) while XP shows the raw gap.
- User's per-minute stats fetched on-demand via Riot Match v5 Timeline API.
- Benchmark stats queried from timeline_snapshots in Neon filtered by
  role and/or champion, rank IN (CHALLENGER, GRANDMASTER, MASTER).

### Success Criteria

- Produces a full OP.GG-style profile
- Shows per-minute stat gaps vs Master+ benchmark
- Filter works across champion and role combinations

# MVP 5: AI Coach

## Goal

Turn statistical findings into natural language coaching.

### Input

Structured metrics:

```json
{
  "champion": "Aatrox",
  "cs10": 58,
  "benchmark_cs10": 72
}
```

### Output

```text
Your laning phase is currently your biggest weakness.
Master-level Aatrox players average 72 CS by 10 minutes, while you average 58.
Improving your farming efficiency could significantly increase your gold income and carry potential.
```

### Success Criteria

Readable coaching reports.

# Future Vision

Long-term, Heimer becomes:

> "ChatGPT for League of Legends improvement."

A player should be able to ask:

"Why am I hard stuck Emerald?"

and receive a data-driven answer based on:

- Thousands of their games
- Millions of benchmark data points
- Champion-specific comparisons
- Patch-specific trends

The system should identify:

- Mechanical weaknesses
- Macro weaknesses
- Champion pool issues
- Vision habits
- Objective control issues
- Snowball conversion issues

and produce actionable coaching recommendations.

---

# Technical North Star

For every recommendation generated by Heimer:

1. It must be backed by data.
2. It must be benchmarked against stronger players.
3. It must be actionable.
4. It must be personalized.
5. It must explain why the recommendation matters.
